FAQs

1. What is UI/UX design, and why is it important?

UI (User Interface) and UX (User Experience) design are crucial for creating effective digital products. UI focuses on the look and feel of a product, including layout, colors, and typography, while UX centers on the overall experience, including usability and user satisfaction. Both are essential for ensuring that products are visually appealing and user-friendly.
